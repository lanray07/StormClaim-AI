StormClaim AI helps roofers, contractors, property managers, landlords, homeowners, and insurance claim support teams document possible storm damage with a clear, professional workflow.

Create a storm damage case, add property and client details, attach roof or interior photos, label damage locations, review cautious AI-assisted findings, organize evidence, and export a clean PDF report for client or claim-support documentation.

Key features:
- Storm damage case creation
- Property and client details
- Camera and photo upload support
- Damage labels for roof, gutter, flashing, leaks, ceiling stains, debris, and exterior concerns
- Mock AI-assisted visible damage findings
- Human approval controls for findings
- Evidence organizer with captions, timestamps, and review status
- Severity badges and repair priority notes
- Native PDF report export and sharing
- Clear insurance, legal, safety, and professional review disclaimers

StormClaim AI is documentation support only. It does not guarantee storm causation, repair cost, insurance coverage, claim approval, legal outcome, structural safety, or inspection certification. AI-assisted findings must be reviewed by qualified professionals. Users should not climb roofs or take unsafe photos.
