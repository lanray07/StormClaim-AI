# StormClaim AI Privacy Policy

StormClaim AI is designed for local storm damage documentation workflows.

## Data Stored By The App

The app stores storm cases, property details, client details, notes, photos, AI-assisted findings, report drafts, generated PDF locations, and subscription state locally on the user's device using SwiftData and local file storage.

## Photos And Camera

StormClaim AI requests camera and photo library access so users can capture or import storm damage evidence for their own cases.

## AI Processing

Mock AI mode is enabled by default. The included remote AI endpoint is a placeholder and should be connected only to a secure backend. API keys must never be stored inside the iOS app.

## Payments

Subscription scaffolding uses StoreKit 2. Payment processing is handled by Apple.

## Third-Party Sharing

The app can export or share generated PDF reports when the user chooses to do so. Users control what they export or share.

## Limitations

StormClaim AI is documentation support only. It is not insurance advice, legal advice, a claim approval tool, a certified roof inspection, or structural engineering advice.
